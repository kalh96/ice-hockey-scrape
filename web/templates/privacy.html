Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

This site does not collect, store, or process any personal data about its visitors. There is no user registration, no contact forms, and no tracking analytics.

2. Cookies

This site does not use cookies or any client-side tracking technologies.

3. Server Logs

This site is hosted on Render. Render may collect standard web server log data (such as IP addresses, request paths, and timestamps) as part of their infrastructure. This data is retained in accordance with Render's Privacy Policy.

4. Cloudflare

This site's domain is managed through Cloudflare. Cloudflare acts as a reverse proxy and may process request metadata (including IP addresses) for security and performance purposes. Please refer to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Your Rights

As we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. If you have any concerns, please contact us via the SIHA community channels.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
