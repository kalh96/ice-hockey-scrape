About

Welcome to Scottish Ice Hockey — an independent website dedicated to covering ice hockey in Scotland, with a focus on the Scottish National League (SNL) and Scottish Cup.

This site publishes articles, match analysis, and live statistics drawn directly from the Scottish Ice Hockey Association (SIHA). All stats are sourced from the SIHA website and updated regularly throughout the season.

The Stats

Statistics are collected automatically from the SIHA website and stored in a local database. This includes fixtures and results, player stats (goals, assists, penalties, saves), team standings, and special-teams data.

While every effort is made to keep stats accurate and up to date, this site is unofficial and the SIHA website should be treated as the authoritative source. Please refer to the Terms of Service for more detail.

Contact

For queries or feedback, please reach out via the SIHA community channels.
