Welcome to **Scottish Ice Hockey** — a new site dedicated to covering ice hockey in Scotland, with a focus on the Scottish National League (SNL) and Scottish Cup.

## What This Site Covers

Throughout the 2025-26 season you can expect:

- **Match reports and analysis** covering SNL and Scottish Cup fixtures
- **Live statistics** — standings, points scorers, netminder stats, and results, updated after each round of games
- **Season previews and reviews** for each team

## The Stats

All statistics are scraped directly from the [SIHA website](https://www.siha-uk.co.uk) and kept up to date throughout the season. The data includes:

- Full league standings (GP, W, L, OTL, GF, GA, GD, PTS)
- Individual player stats — goals, assists, points, and penalty minutes
- Netminder stats — save percentage, goals against average, and time on ice
- Per-match period breakdowns and player stats where available

## The Season So Far

The 2025-26 SNL season is well underway. Head to the [Statistics](/statistics/) page for the current standings, or check [Fixtures & Results](/fixtures/) for the latest scores.
